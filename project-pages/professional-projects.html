Climate Change Consultant with a focus on Hydrology

Independent


Highlights


- Assess the impact of climate change on precipitation, average temperature, maximum temperature and minimum temperature.
- Apply statistical downscaling methods: Quantile Delta Mapping (QDM) ( Cannon, A. 2015 ) and Delta Change ( Teutschbein, C., & Seibert, J. 2012 ).
- Generate ensambles of climate models using climate change scenarios of Representative Concentration Pathways (RCP) and Shared Socioeconomic Pathways (SSP) for future periods of interest.
- Impact of climate change on precipitation and temperature for the analysis of water potential and extreme events.

To achieve the goals, I updated the tool:
- dClimate an application to automate the process of statistical downscaling regional climate models CORDEX with QDM and Delta Change.
- SD_DC_NEX-GDDP and SD_QDM_NEX-GDDP application to automate the process of statistical downscaling of NEX-GDDP-CMIP6 models.

Technical skills


- Languages: Python and R.


- Python Libraries: NumPy, Pandas, Xarray, NetCDF, ee (API Google Earth Engine), Matplotlib, Plotly, Tkinter


- Version Control Systems: Git and GitHub.


- Development Tools: Visual Studio Code, Google Colab, Spyder and Rstudio
