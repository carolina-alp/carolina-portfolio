Volunteer Activities


Description

I participated in several volunteer activities, as a scout in several campaigns and in several camps for about five years. Then, I volunteered as a logistic volunteer for the 2018 South American Games held in my country. In addition, I participated as a volunteer during the period of COVID-19 as a UN volunteer, supporting an online research doing for Paramos Colombia geospatial data management.
Last year I had two amazing experiences, I was a volunteer at Climatematch, as a consultant in basic Python programming, focusing on climate analysis and helping to process data and verify information. Last but not least, I was a member of auto-evaluation commite of the Civil Engineering career of Universidad mayor de San Simon, I collaborate in activities to accredit the career to the Regional Accreditation System for Higher Education in MERCORSUR, ARCU-SUR.
