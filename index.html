About me

I'm a Civil Engineer graduated from Universidad Mayor de San Simón. During my studies I worked as a research assistant in the Hydraulics Laboratory of the Universidad Mayor de San Simón.


With a background in hydrology and climate-related research, I am currently working as a water resources consultant. My expertise includes hydrological modeling, reservoir simulation, downscaling of climate change data from global/regional climate models, and comprehensive data analysis of hydroclimatological information.


As a self-taught Python programmer, I have created scripts that automate tasks to fulfill my professional responsibilities. I am committed to fostering collaboration through open-source contributions on GitHub. Recently, I've just finished a post-graduate course in Machine Learning Cloud at Universidad Católica Boliviana.


Last but not least, I actively participate in various volunteer programs, such as: Climatematch , and ONU Volunteers . These experiences not only refine my personal skills, but also serve as a platform for expanding my professional network, reinforcing my commitment to making meaningful contributions in both my career and the broader community.
